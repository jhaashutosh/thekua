Our Story

Born from a Love of Perfect Fit

ThreadCraft was founded in 2012 by master tailor Arjun Mehta, who believed that every person deserves clothing that fits like a second skin ‚Äî not clothing that they have to fit into.

What began as a single atelier in Mumbai has grown into a beloved brand serving thousands of happy customers across India. But our philosophy has never changed: slow fashion, exceptional craft, and the human touch.

Today, our team of 40+ skilled artisans blends time-honoured tailoring techniques with modern printing technology to create garments that are as individual as the people who wear them.
