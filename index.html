Our Story

From a Small Kitchen
in Bihar to Your Home

What is Thekua and why is it special? +
Thekua is a traditional sweet snack from Bihar, made from whole wheat flour, jaggery (gud), coconut, and ghee. It holds deep cultural significance ‚Äî especially during Chhath Puja, Bihar's most revered festival, where Thekua is offered as Prasad to the Sun God. What makes it special is not just the taste, but the tradition. Each piece is pressed with carved wooden moulds called "thekua sancha" and fried to a golden crisp.
Are your products free of preservatives? +
Absolutely. We use zero artificial preservatives, colours, or flavours. Our Thekua is made with 100% natural ingredients ‚Äî whole wheat atta, pure jaggery, fresh coconut, and desi ghee. The natural preservation comes from the frying process and the jaggery itself, which gives our Thekua a shelf life of 21 days.
